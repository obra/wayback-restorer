# Legal and Rights Notes

## Scope

This project only recovers publicly accessible historical captures for preservation and reader access continuity.

## Boundaries

- No bypass of access controls.
- No recovery of private/admin systems.
- No creation of new editorial comic content.
- No misrepresentation of reconstructed or inferred artifacts as original captures.

## Attribution and Credit

- Preserve creator/site credit as present in recovered material.
- Retain publication context and source attribution where available.

## Rights Questions for Owner Review

- Public mirror publication terms for recovered content.
- Any requested takedown or exclusion handling policy.
- Whether specific third-party assets should be withheld or replaced.
- Trademark/logo usage boundaries for the hosted mirror.
